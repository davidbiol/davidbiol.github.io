About Me

Who am I?

I'm just a curious student from Manizales, Colombia

I think that we all have different opportunities and each one achieves those things that needed to be achieved. In that way, since I was a child I was more focused on what I needed to achieve than on the opportunities I had... maybe saying I achieved is a bit selfish, actually it's a we achieved, I believe that everyone and everything have played a fundamental role in this game. God has given me health and skills, with a family that has supported me, friends who have advised me, teachers who have trusted me and, as I said, everyone who has helped me in this race - It includes you, the person who is reading this website, I appreciate your interest and your time and I hope you really enjoy it.

Among plants and other wonderful things

We must learn to think not only logically but bio-logically
Edward Abbey

Currently I'm studying biology at University of Caldas and I'm in my sixth semester. Since I was younger I knew that I wanted to study life, hence I study biology. Initially I was only interested in genetics, however as I was learning more I realized that everything interested me. And so I like to know everything, from taxonomy and conservation to genetics and cell signaling. The greatest challenge was deciding which living group was the best to study all the topics I liked... after a few months thinking I decided to study plants.
